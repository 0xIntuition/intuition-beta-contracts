Git Source

Inherits: Initializable, Ownable2StepUpgradeable, Multicall3

Creates a triple with a new atom based on the provided atom URI in a single transaction, in situations where two of the atoms comprising the triple are known, and the third atom is new Example use case: First two atoms are known, e.g. "I" and "follow", and the third atom is the user to follow
