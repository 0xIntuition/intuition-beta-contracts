Intuition Beta Contracts Documentation

Attestoor

Git Source

Inherits: Initializable, Ownable2StepUpgradeable

Author: 0xIntuition

A utility contract of the Intuition protocol. It allows for the whitelisted accounts to attest on behalf of the Intuiton itself, effectively acting as an official attestoor account.
